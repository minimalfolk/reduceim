Frequently Asked Questions

Is ReducePic really free?

Yes! ReducePic is completely free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. We may offer premium features in the future, but core compression will always remain free.

How does browser compression work?

Your images are processed entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This means your files never get uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and faster processing.

What's the maximum image size?

You can compress images up to 25MB. For best performance with large images, we recommend using Chrome or Firefox on a computer.
