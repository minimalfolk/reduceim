Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reducing image file sizes

What image formats does ReducePic support? +

ReducePic supports all major image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, and BMP. You can compress any of these formats to reduce their file size.

How much can I reduce my image file size? +

The amount of compression depends on the original image. Typically, you can reduce file sizes by 50-80% without noticeable quality loss. For exact size requirements (like 100KB for job applications), you can set the precise target size.

Is there a limit to how many images I can compress? +

You can compress up to 10 images at once. There's no daily limit - you can use ReducePic as much as you need, completely free.

Will reducing image size affect its quality? +

Our smart compression algorithm finds the optimal balance between file size and quality. For most images, the quality reduction is minimal and often unnoticeable, especially for web use and document submissions.

How long are my images stored on your servers? +

Your privacy is important to us. All uploaded images are automatically deleted from our servers after 30 minutes. We don't store or share your images with any third parties.

Can I use ReducePic on mobile devices? +

Yes! ReducePic is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can compress images directly from your mobile browser.
